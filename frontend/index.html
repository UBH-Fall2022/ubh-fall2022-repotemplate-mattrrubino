About Us

Project team “Code Red” won the UB Hackathon 2020 - 2020 $2,000 Grand Prize. “Code Red” is a team of two Electrical Engineering majors and two Computer Science majors Marcus Francisco, Alexander Francisco, Aditya Pandya, and Matthew Rubino. Our projects over the years has featured progressively more advanced home protection and security systems. This year, the team has pivoted and created a sustainability project known as “Code Green” under the alias of “Code Red”. To illustrate the subset of technology involving manufacturing rather than emergency systems, the team is working under the “Code Blue” name.
